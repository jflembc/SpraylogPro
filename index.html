Connect Google Drive

Connect your Google Drive to upload spray records as CSV files directly to your Drive folder. Records are saved as FieldName_LegalDescription_Date_Commodity.csv

WHAT GETS UPLOADED
✓ Applicator name & cert number
✓ Products applied with EPA numbers & rates
✓ Application date, time, acres, legal description
✓ Weather conditions at time of application
✓ Pest controlled & commodity
ⓘ SprayLog Pro will only be able to create and read files it uploads. It cannot access other files in your Drive.
